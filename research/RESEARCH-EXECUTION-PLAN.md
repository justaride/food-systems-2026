# Research Execution Plan: Deep Research Subagents

**Objective:** Execute high-priority research missions using specialized "Deep Research" prompts from the project's library.
**Methodology:** Subagent-Driven Development. Each task is independent and will be executed by a fresh subagent with web access.

---

## Tasks

### Task 1: Komplett Aktørkart (Norsk matsystem)
- **Prompt Source:** `interessenter-no-aktorkart`
- **Goal:** Map all relevant organizations (regulators, industry bodies, producers, civil society, research groups) in the Norwegian food system.
- **Output File:** `research/interviews/aktorkart-systematisk-2026.md`
- **Context:** Required for Evidence Pack #3 (Stakeholder Map).

### Task 2: Nordisk Regulatorisk Sammenligning
- **Prompt Source:** `reg-en-eu-utp` & `reg-en-competition-law`
- **Goal:** Compare UTP (Unfair Trading Practices) implementation and competition law frameworks across Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland, and the UK.
- **Output File:** `research/regulatory/nordic-regulatorisk-sammenligning-2026.md`
- **Context:** Required for Whitepaper §5 and Evidence Pack #5.

### Task 3: Nordisk Markedsstruktur (CR3/HHI)
- **Prompt Source:** `nordisk-en-market-structure`
- **Goal:** Quantitative comparison of grocery market concentration, store density, and international competition across the Nordics.
- **Output File:** `research/norden/nordisk-markedsstruktur-data-2026.md`
- **Context:** Required for Whitepaper §4 and Mission 2.

### Task 4: Selvforsyning og Beredskapsmodeller
- **Prompt Source:** `nordisk-en-self-sufficiency`
- **Goal:** Compare caloric self-sufficiency rates and strategic reserve models (especially the Finnish NESA model) across the Nordics.
- **Output File:** `research/norden/nordisk-selvforsyning-beredskap-2026.md`
- **Context:** Required for Whitepaper §4.2 and Mission 4.
